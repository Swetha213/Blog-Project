Places I visited in USA

South Beach, Miami Florida

No matter what this beach will be special to me, As this is the first beach I visted in USA. I always wanted to vist miami as I am very fond of beaches. And I recommend this beach as a must vist escpecially to all the beach lovers like me. If you want you can also try beach activities like parasailing and jet ski.

Chicago, Illinois

If anyone asks me which place is close to your heart in USA, I would say Chicago, because this is the first city I ever visted in USA, and this is the first city I went to with love of my life and I cherish that forever. Apart from everything I would recommend everyone to visit once in their lives. It is very beautiful and it has the second largest downtown in USA.

Universal Studios, Orlando, Florida

I am the type of person who is a kid at heart and still fond of all the cartoon stuff. This the place where you can enjoy every single ride and will get chance to do some adventurous stuff. I personally loved the Hulk,Minion and Harry potter rides and enjoyed them to the fullest. I recommend this place to all the people not only kids even adults will enjoy this park and will have a great time.

Rocky Mountain National Park, Colorado

If you are a type of person who loves to hike even in winters, then you must visit this place which is located in colorado and it leaves you with breathtaking view and best place with adventures for hiking. But one thing for sure make sure you are ready for the extremely lower temperture with all the gadgets that are necessary for you to keep you warm.

Smoky Mountains, Tennessee

If you are a type of person who are fond of colors. Then please make a vist to this during fall time in USA and you can enjoy and the view and place with beautiful fall colors around you. If you have enough time try to take a train ride around the moutains and enjoy the view.
